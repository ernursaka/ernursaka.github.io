Senior Data Scientist — Personalization, Recommendation Systems, Machine Learning

I build machine learning systems for live-service games, including personalization, recommendation, gameplay modeling, and LLM-based tooling.

My work focuses on systems that are used in production — replacing external solutions, supporting content and product workflows, and helping teams understand and act on model outputs.

Selected Industry Work

Generative AI – Objective Creation

Built an LLM-based system that converts natural language into structured JSON for live-service objective creation, with validation and grounding to ensure outputs are usable by producers.

Impact: Used to generate ~82% of FC26 launch objectives (142 objectives) and reduced setup time significantly.

Personalization Systems

Worked on personalization across objectives, store, and ads using segmentation, ranking logic, and experimentation, with a focus on stability and real-world usage.

Impact: Supported targeted experiences and ongoing live-service experimentation.

Recommendation Systems

Built recommendation systems across products, including pack recommendation for SimCity BuildIt and game recommendation for a subscription platform.

Impact: Replaced an external vendor system and scaled recommendations to real player usage.

Gameplay Modeling – xG

Developed an xG model focused on spatial realism and goalkeeper interaction, with emphasis on understanding how and why predictions differ across gameplay scenarios.

Impact: Enabled evaluation based on model behavior, not just aggregate metrics.

ML Tools & Decision Support

Built internal tools, including a Streamlit-based xG application, to allow producers and engineers to explore predictions, analyze scenarios, and investigate edge cases.

Impact: Made models accessible and usable in cross-team decision-making.
