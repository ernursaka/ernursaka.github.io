About

I am a Data Scientist at Electronic Arts building large-scale machine learning and AI systems for live services, including personalization, recommendation systems, gameplay modeling, and LLM-powered content generation.

My work has delivered multi-million dollar impact across multiple titles, including replacing external recommendation systems, enabling large-scale personalization, and deploying AI-assisted content generation used in production workflows.

I focus on building production-ready systems that go beyond model accuracy, emphasizing real-world behavior, usability, and measurable business outcomes across data science, product, and engineering teams.

Due to confidentiality, implementation details and source code for industry projects are not publicly available. Descriptions below focus on system design, modeling approach, and impact.

Selected Industry Projects

Generative AI – Objective Creation

Designed and delivered an LLM-based objective generation system that converts natural-language concepts into structured JSON for live-service tooling. The workflow combined prompt orchestration, schema-based validation, retrieval-based grounding, and producer-facing controls to make AI-assisted content creation usable in practice.

Impact: Enabled AI-assisted creation of ~82% of FC26 launch objectives (142 objectives) and reduced setup time by ~60%.

Personalization Systems – Objectives, Ads, and Store

Built and supported personalization systems across multiple FC live-service surfaces, including targeted objectives, ads recommendation, and store experimentation. This work combined segmentation, ranking logic, A/B testing, and rollout validation to make personalization scalable and safe to operate.

Impact: Supported systems with multi-million-dollar revenue contribution, including projected revenue impact for targeted objectives and measurable uplifts in live experimentation.

Recommendation Systems – SimCity BuildIt and Subscription Platform

Developed recommendation systems across multiple products, including a pack recommendation system for SimCity BuildIt and a game recommendation system for a subscription platform. The work covered ranking design, feature engineering, pipeline support, experiment analysis, and production deployment.

Impact: Replaced an outsourced recommendation solution, generated multi-million-dollar revenue, and scaled recommendations to a large share of the player base.

Gameplay Modeling – Expected Goals (xG)

Developed a next-generation xG model for EA Sports FC with a stronger focus on spatial realism, including goalkeeper–shooter interaction and scenario-level behavioral analysis. Rather than treating model evaluation as only a metric comparison problem, the work focused on understanding when models diverge, why they diverge, and which gameplay situations drive those differences.

Impact: Improved confidence in model behavior, highlighted weaknesses in legacy logic, and supported a shift from metric-based validation to behavior-based evaluation.

Decision Support & Internal ML Tools

Built internal tools to help producers and gameplay engineers inspect model outputs, investigate scenarios, and better understand prediction behavior. This included stakeholder-facing analysis workflows and interactive tools used to explore xG predictions, edge cases, and model explainability in a more practical way.

Impact: Made model behavior more accessible to non-DS stakeholders and improved collaboration between data science, producers, and engineering.

Additional Applied ML Work

Worked across a broader set of applied ML problems including churn prediction, retention analysis, NLP-based keyword extraction, OCR/content moderation exploration, game economy simulation, player pricing, and advertising effectiveness modeling.

Impact: Expanded the use of predictive modeling and analysis across product, gameplay, and content workflows while supporting experimentation and decision-making in different game contexts.
