About

I am a Data Scientist at Electronic Arts working on machine learning systems, recommendation engines, personalization, experimentation, and Generative AI applications for live services.

My work focuses on building production-ready systems that improve player experience, support content creation workflows, and deliver measurable business impact.

Note: Due to confidentiality, implementation details and source code for industry projects are not publicly available. Descriptions here focus on high-level system design and impact.

Selected Industry Projects

Generative AI – Objective Creation

Built an LLM-based system that converts natural language into structured JSON for live-service objective creation. Designed the workflow with schema validation and retrieval-based grounding to ensure reliable outputs.

Impact: Enabled ~82% of FC26 launch objectives and reduced content setup time by ~60%.

Personalization Systems (Objectives, Ads, Store)

Developed personalization systems across objectives, store, and ads using segmentation, ranking logic, and experimentation. Designed pipelines supporting scalable rollout across live services.

Impact: Delivered measurable conversion improvements and multi-million dollar revenue impact.

Recommendation Systems (SimCity & Subscription)

Built and deployed recommendation systems across multiple titles, including a pack recommendation system for SimCity BuildIt and a game recommendation system for a subscription platform.

Impact: Generated multi-million dollar revenue, replaced external solutions, and scaled to a large player base.

Gameplay Modeling – xG System

Developed a next-generation xG model focused on spatial realism and goalkeeper–shooter interaction, improving how shot quality is evaluated across gameplay scenarios.

Impact: Improved model behavior understanding and supported a shift from metric-based validation to behavior-based evaluation.

Decision Support & ML Tools

Built internal tools for producers and gameplay engineers to explore model predictions, analyze scenarios, and understand outputs through interactive interfaces.

Impact: Enabled non-technical stakeholders to inspect models, debug edge cases, and support gameplay decisions.
