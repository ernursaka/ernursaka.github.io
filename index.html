About

Selected Industry Projects

Generative AI Objective Generation

Built an LLM-based system that converts natural language into structured JSON for live-service objective creation. Designed the workflow with schema validation and retrieval-based grounding for more reliable outputs.

Impact: Enabled ~82% of FC26 launch objectives (142 objectives) and reduced setup time by ~60%.

Recommendation & Personalization

Developed recommendation systems for targeted objectives, store personalization, and ads using segmentation, experimentation, and ranking logic.

Impact: Supported systems with projected and measured multi-million dollar revenue impact across FC live services.

Machine Learning Systems

Built and deployed production ML systems including pack recommendation, churn prediction, xG modeling, and gameplay-related analysis across multiple titles.

Impact: Replaced external solutions, improved player targeting, and generated multi-million dollar business value.

SimCity BuildIt Recommendation

Built and deployed a machine learning-based pack recommendation system that replaced an external vendor solution. The system ranked large candidate sets using player behavior and game context.

Impact: Generated multi-million dollar revenue, achieved strong uplift, and scaled to a large share of the player base.

Ads & Store Experimentation

Worked on recommendation logic, experimentation design, and measurement for ads and store personalization systems, including A/B testing and bandit-style approaches.

Impact: Contributed to measurable conversion and revenue improvements in live systems.

xG, Churn, and Modeling

Built and validated predictive models for gameplay and player behavior, including xG models, churn prediction, and explainability analysis.

Impact: Improved confidence in model behavior and supported better product and gameplay decisions.
