Senior Data Scientist — End-to-End ML Systems, Recommendation Systems, Generative AI

I build end-to-end machine learning systems for live-service games, covering data collection, feature engineering, modeling, deployment, experimentation, and product integration.

My work includes replacing outsourced recommendation systems with in-house solutions, shipping LLM-based tooling used in production content workflows, and building tools that help producers and engineers understand and act on model outputs.

Experience includes recommendation systems, personalization, gameplay modeling, experimentation, and LLM-based content generation.

Selected Industry Work

Generative AI – Objective Creation

Designed and deployed an LLM-based system that converts natural-language objective concepts into structured JSON for live-service tooling. Built validation, grounding, and producer-facing controls to make AI-generated outputs usable in practice.

Impact: Used to generate the majority of launch objectives and reduced setup time by more than half.

Personalization Systems – Objectives, Ads, and Store

Built and supported personalization systems across targeted objectives, store surfaces, and ads using segmentation, ranking logic, experimentation, and rollout validation.

Impact: Supported production personalization systems used across objectives, ads, and store, enabling targeted experiences and ongoing experimentation in live environments.

Recommendation Systems – SimCity BuildIt and Subscription Platform

Developed recommendation systems across multiple products, including a pack recommendation system for SimCity BuildIt and a game recommendation system for a subscription platform.

Impact: Replaced an outsourced recommendation solution, brought recommendation ownership in-house, and delivered consistent performance improvements with about 7–11% uplift across key scenarios.

Gameplay Modeling – Expected Goals (xG)

Developed multiple xG models for EA Sports FC, including pre-shot and post-shot versions, with a stronger focus on spatial realism, feature design, and goalkeeper interaction.

Impact: Improved xG prediction quality, supported deployment through ONNX generation, and helped shift evaluation from aggregate metrics toward scenario-level model behavior and explainability.

Decision Support & Internal ML Tools

Built internal tools, including a Streamlit-based xG application, to allow producers and engineers to inspect predictions, explore scenarios, investigate edge cases, and better understand model behavior.

Impact: Made model outputs directly usable in cross-functional decision-making instead of limiting analysis to offline DS workflows.

Additional Applied ML Work

Worked across a broader set of applied ML problems including churn prediction, retention analysis, player pricing, NLP-based keyword extraction, OCR and content moderation exploration, advertising effectiveness modeling, and game economy simulation.

Impact: Extended predictive modeling and data-driven decision support into multiple gameplay, product, and content workflows.
