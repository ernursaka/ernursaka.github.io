Mapping Tool

The focus of my research is using computer science to find clusters of substrings (or probes) that come from the same gene for focused bioinformatics research such as understanding genomic knowledge about Alzheimer. Before the package I prepared, the suggested clusters were only on the gene and transcript level, with my tool, mapping level is more granular, in terms of untranslated region (3'-UTR, 5'-UTR) and exon. This opens the opportunity for new discoveries using old data and recent advancements in bioinformatics..

The tool as a R package can be reached from https://github.com/UofLBioinformatics/affyCustomCDF

The output files produced via affyCustomCdf tool for four organisms can be reached from http://bioinformatics.louisville.edu/RegionCDFDesc.html
